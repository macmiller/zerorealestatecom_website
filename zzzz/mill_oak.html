Mill Oak Remodel: This project was to rehab a house which had been used as a rental for many years. This was before I became a fan of laminate flooring, so I just installed new carpet in the house. The kitchen cabinets were refinished. If doing it today I would rip out the existing kitchen and install a new kitchen with granite counters. There was a full paint done including the ceilings. The old roof was stripped off and a new 30 year shingle roof was installed. Also seamless white gutters were put on.
The project looked nice when completed and resulted in 3 over full price offers in the first few days. The price was pushing the envelope as far as appraisals, but we did get the contract price.
