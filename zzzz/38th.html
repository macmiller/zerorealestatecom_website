38th Remodel:  This was an expensive and time consuming remodel.  First off all the house was full of stuff when I got it.  Secondly, there was a non permitted structure in the back, which needed to have a new foundation put on (basically the entire back room was rebuilt).  The electric in the house had to be totally re-done, with a new panel being installed.  The kitchen was replaced.  The flooring was replaced with laminate throughout.

Some before video walk throughs-->   

This project required an architect and constant trips to the City Building department.
